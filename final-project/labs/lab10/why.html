Why is it important?

The EPUB standard is important because it solved problems in the digital publishing industry. It is the industry standard for digital content like books and magazines. The EPUP format is open source, making it available to any and everyone. No single company owns it who can restrict who uses it.

Another reason it is important is because it is flexible and accesible. It uses familiar web technologies like HTML, CSS, and XML. Content can adapt to different screen sizes and devices. It supports screen readers, adjustable font, and navigation for people with visual or other impariments.

Lastly, it helps in long term preservation. Physical books can be damaged, out of print, and many are loss to time. By storing books digitally, books can be preserved for future generations. Because EPUP is open source and not proprietary, it doesn't rely on a single company to maintain it.
